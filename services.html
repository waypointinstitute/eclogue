Hardscape services built for Bend, OR

Every install is engineered for freeze/thaw, slope, and drainage so the finished surface stays level for years.

Stonemasonry

Retaining walls, seat walls, fire features, outdoor kitchens, and veneers—each hand-set on a reinforced base.

  • Basalt, granite, and natural stone sourced locally
  • Drainage and geogrid tiebacks engineered for slopes
  • Integrated lighting and caps for year-round use

Paver patios & driveways

Freeze/thaw-ready patios, driveways, and pool decks installed with polymeric sand and edging that resists movement.

  • 3-step compaction with open graded base for drainage
  • Permeable options for stormwater compliance
  • Sealing and maintenance plan with every install

Walkways & paths

Durable paths with consistent joints, slope control, and tidy transitions to turf, mulch, or plantings.

  • Accessible slopes and slip-resistant materials
  • Edge restraint and drainage to protect lawns
  • Integration with lighting and irrigation

Our process

1. Site consult

We walk the property, assess grade, and capture elevations to plan drainage and material transitions.

2. Engineering the base

Compaction, geotextile, and edge restraint deliver stability through Bend winters.

3. Finishing & handoff

Clean cuts, tight joints, and a maintenance guide keep your investment performing.
