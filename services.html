What we build

Choose the scope that fits your home. Each service pairs craftsmanship with the engineering needed for the high desert.

Stonemasonry

Retaining walls, seat walls, fire features, and veneers hand-set on reinforced bases with drainage and geogrid.

  • Basalt, granite, and natural stone sourced locally
  • Geogrid and drain tile engineered for slopes
  • Lighting, caps, and finish work for year-round use
Paver patios & driveways

Patios, pool decks, and driveways installed with open-graded base, polymeric sand, and snow-ready edge restraint.

  • Permeable and traditional options
  • Three-stage compaction with geotextile
  • Sealing and maintenance guides included
Walkways & paths

Accessible paths and garden walks with consistent slope, tidy transitions, and integrated lighting.

  • Slip-resistant finishes and grading
  • Edge restraint to protect turf and beds
  • Irrigation and lighting coordination

Project add-ons

Outdoor kitchens

Water features

Basalt bubbling boulders, reflective pools, and recirculating systems built for minimal maintenance.

Lighting plans

Low-voltage lighting layouts highlighting steps, paths, and architectural focal points.
