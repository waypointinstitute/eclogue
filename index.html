The Wrenley process

01

Site consult

Walk-through, measure grade, and map drainage to plan base depth, steps, and transitions.

02

Engineering the base

Excavation, geotextile, compaction, and rigid edge systems create a foundation that stays true.

03

Stone & paver install

Hand-set stone, tight joints, and specialty cuts customized to the patterns you select.

04

Finishing & handoff

Lighting, sealing, joint sanding, and a maintenance guide to protect your investment.
